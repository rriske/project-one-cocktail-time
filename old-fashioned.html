Old Fashioned



Ingredients

  • 2 oz Bourbon
  • Splash of Club Soda
  • 4 Dashes Bitters
  • Sugar cube
  • Ice

Garnish

  • Cherry
  • Orange peel

Created in the early 1900â€™s, the Old Fashioned was the first widespread attempt to make liquor taste better. This become one of the first known cocktails and paved the way for modern day drink mixing.
1 To make this classic, you begin by muddling the sugar cube and bitters in the bottom of an old fashioned glass
2 Add ice and pour in your bourbon of choice
3 Stir well and top with a splash of club soda
4 Garnish with a cherry and orange peel and enjoy!
