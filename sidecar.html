Sidecar


Ingredients

  • 2 oz Cognac
  • 1 oz Cointreau orange liqueur
  • 3/4 oz Lemon juice

Garnish

  • Orange twist

Can use cognac, Armagnac or bourbon.
1 This classic cocktail is made by putting all ingredients into a shaker with ice
2 Give it a good shake and strain the mix into a chilled cocktail class
3 Garnish with an orange twist and enjoy!
